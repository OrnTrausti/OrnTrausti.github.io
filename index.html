Why you should play D&D

Have you ever played “Let’s Pretend” and found it fun? Dungeons and Dragons is like Let’s Pretend, but with rules as scaffolding to build bigger (if clunkier) structures so everyone can agree who hit who and you can have games that last literal years.

Have you ever played an MMO and thought “I love my guild but I hate all these jerks and griefers on the server”? Dungeons and Dragons is like an MMO where you trade off crap graphics for just playing with cool people.

Have you ever played an MMO or computer RPG and wanted to try an off the wall strategy the coders hadn’t thought of? Because in Dungeons & Dragons you have a human GM who lets you do things like that.

Have you ever played a dungeon in a computer RPG and wanted to design your own but not had the programming skill? Because you don’t need to be able to program to GM.
